How It Works

From download to dinner, in four simple steps.

1

Download the App

Get Platify for free from the Google Play Store. Create your account in seconds and you're ready to go.

2

Add Your Recipes

Build your personal cookbook. Add ingredients, step-by-step directions, cover images, and nutrition info. Organise recipes into boards like "Weeknight Dinners" or "Italian Favourites" and tag them for quick filtering.

3

Track Your Nutrition

Use the Records tab to log what you eat throughout the day. Search thousands of products, scan barcodes, or add items from your Collections. Platify tracks calories, carbs, protein, and fat against your personal goals.

4

Share Recipes

Share your favourite recipes with friends and family via a simple link. They can view them in the browser without needing the app.

More things you can do

Collections

Group products together, like "Breakfast" or "Post-Workout", and log them to your diary in one tap.

Personalised Goals

Set daily calorie and macronutrient goals that match your lifestyle and dietary needs.

Privacy First

Your personal data never leaves your phone. only recipes you choose to make public are stored on our server.
